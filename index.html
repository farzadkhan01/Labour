A message from Prime Minister, Keir Starmer

I’d like to say a heartfelt thank you for the support you gave us on Thursday 4 July.

A mandate like this comes with great responsibility. I promise you this: Labour will
govern for the whole country. We will put the chaos of the last 14 years behind us, turn
the page and start to rebuild.
